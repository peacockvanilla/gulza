Trading

After the ICO, we will apply to list ourselves on several external exchanges. An Internal Exchange will also be created within our dashboard for users to buy and sell STRU tokens. In the future, BTC, ETH can also be traded.

Lending

Our lending program has the potential to give you gains of up to 40% per month. You will have the ability to withdraw the daily interest rates during the capital lock period. For detailed info, please refer to the WHITEPAPER.

Referral Program

Through this program, you have a chance to earn bonus tokens for every person who joins our website and makes a purchase through your referral code. We have 3 levels of bonuses: level 1 (@ 5%) Level 2 (@ 3%) Level 3 (@ 1%).

Structured Network Marketing

Spread News, Get Reward! For all you social birds out there, this is the right section for you. We will be rewarding the marketers an additional percentage of their purchased tokens whenever they share a Facebook or Twitter post, share YouTube videos, write blog articles etc. For more info, click the FREE TOKEN tab or click here

Contests & Giveaways

After the ICO ends, we have some unique contests for you that will run for over a year. These contests will take place every 3 months and will give everyone an opportunity to earn free tokens. Only people who are in possession of STRU tokens will be allowed to participate in these contests. For detailed info, please refer to the WHITEPAPER.

Buy & Sell Products

Users will be able to make some extra digital cash for the goods they don’t use. Sell your old stuff by posting an ad on the Structure Marketplace (Coming soon…), and allow buyers to pay for the goods with the BTC/ETH/STRU coins.
For detailed info, please refer to the WHITEPAPER.

Products

Structure mobile app

A mobile app (IOS and Android) containing many features to make your lives easy will be released in the future. The STRU tokens will be used to unlock certain features within the app.

Structure Card

A Structure card will be used to make payments at many stores all over the world. This card will be able to store a few coins such as BTC, STRU, ETH, LTC. This will be integrated along with the Structure Marketplace.

Structure Exchange

This will be the place where users can trade all currencies against each other. Users will pay a fee of 0.05% on all trades. This will be a major source of revenue for building the business.
